VESSEL OVERVIEW

The Wraith Dart is the primary strike craft and culling vehicle of the Wraith fleet. These small, agile fighters are grown within the organic bays of Hive Ships and serve as the sharp edge of Wraith military operations. Unlike conventional fighters, Darts are bio-organic constructs, linked telepathically to their pilot.

Darts are most feared for their culling beams — transport technology capable of scooping up dozens of humans in a single pass. During a culling, entire populations can be harvested within hours as swarms of Darts sweep across a planet's surface.

KAMIKAZE CAPABILITY

Wraith pilots will not hesitate to ram their Darts into enemy targets as a last resort attack.

Damage: Significant on impact

OPERATIONAL HISTORY

Wraith Darts have been the terror of the Pegasus Galaxy for millennia. Every culling in the galaxy's history has been carried out by swarms of these bio-organic fighters, descending on human worlds like a plague.

Notable Actions:

  • Siege of Atlantis — hundreds of Darts launched against the Ancient city
  • Culling of Athos and countless other human worlds
  • Engagements against F-302 fighters in Pegasus orbit
  • Kamikaze strikes against Tau'ri warships
  • Reconnaissance operations through Stargates

TACTICAL ANALYSIS

The Wraith Dart's strength lies in numbers. Individually fragile compared to shielded fighters, Darts compensate through overwhelming swarm tactics. A single Hive Ship can deploy hundreds of Darts simultaneously, saturating enemy defenses and creating openings for larger vessels.

Recommended Counter-Tactics:

  • Concentrated point-defense fire from rail gun batteries
  • F-302 interceptors in defensive screen formation
  • Asgard beam weapons for rapid swarm elimination
  • Ancient drone weapons for precision strikes
